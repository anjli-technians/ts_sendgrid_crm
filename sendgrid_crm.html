SendGrid CRM

SendGrid can be easily integrated with CRM systems to improve email communication for customer relationship management. With SendGrid, CRM platforms can automate the delivery of customized emails, such as transactional, promotional, and engagement emails. Through its robust API and SMTP relay, SendGrid provides high deliverability and real-time analytics, enabling CRM users to monitor open rates, clicks, and user interactions. This information enables businesses to fine-tune their communication strategy and enhance customer engagement. SendGrid's scalability, email templates, and security features make it an excellent utility for CRM systems, allowing businesses to provide effective, reliable, and targeted email campaigns.

After entering the API key, you can mark the checkbox to sync CRM leads on creation and deletion and display a warning. You can also set the limit for the maximum number of leads that can be sent at a time.

Open CRM Configuration and select the "Map Field" option to choose which fields you want to map.

Select the field you want to map with SendGrid.

In the action menu, click on "Select Export Lead to SendGrid."

Wizards open and display the SendGrid field that syncs from the lead.

You can export a single lead or multiple leads to SendGrid.

Can my CRM Leads be synced with SendGrid?
Yes, CRM Leads are automatically synced to enhance communication.
Is the data syncing automatic?
Yes, the app will naturally be syncing data from your system to SendGrid.
What does this module do?
This module syncs CRM leads from Odoo to SendGrid, ensuring lead data is available for email marketing and automation.
How does the synchronization work?
The module automatically transfers lead details from Odoo's CRM module to SendGrid whenever a new lead is created or updated.
Which lead fields are synced to SendGrid?
The module allows syncing of key fields such as lead name, email, phone number, company name, and other custom fields.
Can I select which leads to sync with SendGrid?
Yes, you can configure filters or conditions in Odoo to determine which leads should be sent to SendGrid.
